# Codebase Analysis Prompt

Analyze the Cupcake design system codebase with a focus on the following aspects:

1. **Architecture**: Evaluate the overall architecture and organization of the codebase.
2. **Component Patterns**: Identify common patterns and inconsistencies across components.
3. **Styling Approach**: Assess the styling approach and use of design tokens.
4. **Mobile Compatibility**: Evaluate mobile support across the codebase.
5. **Accessibility**: Assess accessibility features and compliance.
6. **Performance**: Identify performance concerns and optimization opportunities.
7. **Testing Coverage**: Evaluate testing coverage and quality.
8. **Documentation**: Assess documentation quality and completeness.

Provide specific recommendations for improvements in each area, with a prioritized list of action items.
